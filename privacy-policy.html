VoxScript Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Overview

VoxScript is designed with your privacy as the top priority. We believe your data is yours, and yours alone. This privacy policy explains our commitment to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

VoxScript does not collect, store, transmit, or process any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Audio or video files you transcribe
  • Transcription text or results
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Location data
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

How VoxScript Works

All transcription processing happens entirely on your device:

  • On-Device Processing: Audio and video files are processed locally using WhisperKit AI technology running on your iPhone's processor.
  • No Cloud Uploads: Your files never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to share or export them using iOS's built-in share functionality.
  • No Internet Required: After the initial download of AI models, VoxScript works completely offline.
  • No Account Required: VoxScript does not require account creation, login, or any form of user registration.

Permissions

VoxScript requests the following iOS permissions to function:

  • Photo Library Access: To allow you to select videos from your photo library for transcription. Files remain on your device.
  • Files Access: To allow you to import audio and video files from the Files app or iCloud Drive. Files remain on your device.

These permissions are used solely for their stated purpose and do not enable any data collection.

Third-Party Services

VoxScript does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external APIs. The app is completely self-contained.

AI Model Downloads

On first use, VoxScript downloads Whisper AI models from Apple's content delivery network. These downloads are handled by iOS and do not involve any data collection by VoxScript. The models are stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

All data created by VoxScript (transcriptions, settings, downloaded models) is stored locally on your device in the app's sandbox. This data is:

  • Only accessible by VoxScript
  • Not backed up to iCloud by default (follows iOS backup policies)
  • Deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Never transmitted to external servers

Exports and Sharing

When you export or share transcriptions:

  • VoxScript uses iOS's native share sheet
  • You control where exported files are sent
  • VoxScript does not have access to or knowledge of where you share files
  • The sharing functionality is provided entirely by iOS

Children's Privacy

VoxScript does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. Since we don't collect data at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and notify users through an app update description.

Your Rights

Since VoxScript does not collect any personal data, there is no data to:

  • Request access to
  • Request deletion of
  • Request correction of
  • Opt out of collection of

Your privacy is protected by design, not by policy alone.

Legal Compliance

VoxScript complies with:

  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Compliance is straightforward because we simply don't collect any data.
